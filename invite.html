What to Bring

Our sessions blend coding / robot building and basketball. The mix varies week to week — Coach G reads the room and adjusts based on how the group is engaging that day. We provide everything for the STEM side (LEGO SPIKE Prime robots, laptops, worksheets).

For the basketball side, your student just needs:

  • Comfortable clothes they can move in
  • Sneakers — no crocs, sandals, or open-toe shoes
  • A water bottle is always a good idea

What to Expect: The SumoBot Challenge

Your student is jumping in during our multi-week SumoBot Challenge — teams design, build, and code robots that battle in a sumo ring. Here's a 30-second preview of what the finished robots look like in action:

▶ Watch preview (YouTube)

How we onboard new students: Your student will be paired with a returning student who has built sumobots before. The veteran student has a formal assignment to walk your student through how the robot hardware works and how the code controls it — so your student learns the fundamentals with a peer, not by playing catch-up on their own.
